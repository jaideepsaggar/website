About AJ Spinning & Textile Mills

Who We Are

Craftsmanship Meets Commerce

AJ Spinning & Textile Mills is a Ludhiana-based B2B textile manufacturer specialising in premium quality woolen shawls and yarns. We serve retailers, wholesalers, exporters and boutiques across India with a comprehensive range of fabric blends and design finishes.

Our manufacturing expertise spans over 10 fabric varieties — from pure wool and silk wool to technical blends like wool nylon and acrowool. We understand that every business has unique requirements, which is why we offer two flexible selling formats: Made to Order and Made to Customisation.

Based in Ludhiana — Punjab's textile manufacturing hub — we have access to the finest raw materials and a skilled workforce that ensures consistent quality across every order.
